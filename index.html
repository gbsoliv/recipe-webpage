Coffee cupcake with chantilly

The perfect little cupcake to enjoy with your daily coffee. It's super easy to make and turns out amazing — it’s sure to impress. Try it out and let me know what you think!

Time: 1h10
Yield: 12 servings
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon of instant coffee
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ cup of oil
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • ⅔ cup of cocoa powder
  • 2 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 2 cups of ready-made Chantilly cream
  • Coffee beans for decoration
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon of instant coffee
  • 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons of butter

Preparation Instructions

In a blender, blend the water, instant coffee, eggs, oil, sugar, and cocoa powder until smooth. Transfer the mixture to a bowl, add the flour and baking powder, and mix with a spoon. Pour the batter into cupcake liners placed inside a large muffin tray, arranging them side by side. Bake in a preheated medium oven (350°F/180°C) for 25 minutes or until baked and golden. Remove from the oven and let cool.

For the filling, combine all the ingredients in a saucepan over medium heat, stirring constantly until thickened. Remove from the heat, let cool, and transfer to a piping bag fitted with a round tip. Set aside. Place the Chantilly cream into another piping bag fitted with a star tip and set aside.

To assemble, make a hole in the center of each cupcake and fill it with the reserved coffee brigadeiro filling. Decorate the tops of the cupcakes with the reserved Chantilly cream, piping in circular motions. Garnish with coffee beans and refrigerate until ready to serve.
